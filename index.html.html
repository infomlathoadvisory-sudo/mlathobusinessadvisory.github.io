Common Questions

Answers to What You're
Probably Wondering

How much do Mlatho's services cost? ▾
Our fees depend on the scope of work and the size of your business. Starting prices range from MWK 50,000 for a business advisory consultation to MWK 75,000/month for basic bookkeeping. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges. The best way to get an accurate quote is through a free initial consultation — we assess your needs first, then recommend the most cost-effective solution.
Do you work with very small businesses and sole traders? ▾
Absolutely. We work with businesses at every stage — from sole traders just starting out to established corporate organisations and NGOs. If you are operating a business in Malawi and need financial or advisory support, Mlatho is built for you.
Can you help us with MRA registration and tax compliance? ▾
Yes. We assist with MRA registration, TPIN application, VAT registration, PAYE management, tax return filing, and ongoing monthly compliance support. We ensure you are fully compliant with Malawi Revenue Authority requirements at all times — so you can focus on running your business.
Do I need to visit your office in Lilongwe? ▾
Not necessarily. While we welcome in-person meetings at our Lilongwe office, we work with many clients remotely via WhatsApp, phone, and email — making our services fully accessible to businesses across Malawi, wherever you are located.
How quickly can you start working with us? ▾
Once we have completed your initial consultation and agreed on the scope of work, we typically begin within days — not weeks. We understand that compliance timelines and business opportunities don't wait, and we operate with the same urgency you need.
What are your payment terms? ▾
Monthly services are paid in advance. One-off projects require a 60% deposit before commencement of work, with the 40% balance due before release of final documents. Urgent work attracts an additional 30–50% surcharge. All prices are starting prices and may vary by scope — we'll give you a full, transparent quotation before any work begins.
